Building the Legacy

Founded in 2011, the team has designed, manufactured, and competed annually at Baja SAE competitions across the continent. At the end of each academic year, our team competes internationally in the Baja SAE collegiate design series. Organized by the Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE) and taking place in the United States, these intercollegiate competitions take place over four days.
