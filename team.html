The drivetrain subteam specializes in transferring power from the motor to the wheels. Our focus lies on the engine, gearing, and transmission as well as hubs, drive shafts and CV joints. Our team members design and optimize parts, perform stress and thermal analysis, collect and analyze data, and conduct hands on testing. We are passionate about what we do and have ambitious projects lined up for this year. Currently, we are looking for members of all experience levels, so if you are interested in learning about or have experience in mechanical design, power transmission, and dynamic testing talk to us today!

The suspension subteam is directly concerned with how the vehicle rides and handles. Parts we work on include linkages such as the control arms at the front, and trailing arms in the rear, the shocks, uprights, steering rack etc. The Suspension team is a great opportunity to learn about vehicle dynamics, 3D CAD Modelling, structural design and design optimizations through Finite Element Analyses, Sheet Metal design, and a variety of practical hands-on skills like mill and lathe work, and welding. If any of these sounds interesting to you, be sure to get in touch with us!

The chassis & driver controls subteam is responsible for operator controls, ergonomics, frame design, and bridging the scope between all other subteams. Projects are highly interdisciplinary and vary widely in complexity, providing member with the opportunity to utilize all their skills. Members can expect to develop knowledge in a range of field including mechanical design & optimization, CNC & manual machining, and advanced composite fabrication. Design projects have included design of the steel frame with torsional rigidity analysis, ergonomic carbon fibre steering wheel and seat, throttle & brake pedals , body panels, and more.

This subteam oversees the design, manufacture, control and analysis of mechatronic and electronic automotive systems in addition to collecting and analysing vehicle data. In doing so we utilize advanced software for 3D modelling, programming, software development and data analysis. We also work hands on with power distribution systems, wire harnesses, data acquisition systems, custom sensors/boards, mechanical parts and assemblies, industrial machining equipment and much more! Other subteams also rely on our expertise to support electrification and testing efforts along the way.
